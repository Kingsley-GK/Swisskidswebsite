About our School

Empowering Young Minds, Building a Bright Future.

Brief History

Welcome to Swiss Kids Academy (The tech school for pupils), where
we embark on a mission to create a world of boundless possibilities for
every child who walks through our doors. Our story is one of passion,
dedication, and a deep-rooted commitment to nurturing young minds.
Here, we strive to be more than just a school; we are a vibrant
community that embraces diversity, curiosity, and the pursuit of
knowledge. At Swiss Kids Academy, we envision a future where every
child discovers their unique strengths and talents, unleashing their
potential to create positive change in the world. We strive to foster an
environment that sparks creativity, ignites passion, and nurtures a love
for learning that lasts a lifetime. Our motto is "Education beyond class"

Our Key Features

Vision

At Swiss Kids Academy, we are determined to
impart knowledge about the process of learning to
children and enable students to
acquire effective learning strategies. To provide a warm, caring and stimulating
atmosphere supported by Christian values
where individual talents are nurtured,
developed and celebrated.

Our Motto

"Education Beyond Class"

Mission

Our mission is to deliver a world class learning fundamentals to our children. We empower each student with knowledge, critical thinking skills, and a passion for learning that extends beyond the classroom.

Ethos

At Swiss Kids Academy, our core ethos are : professionalism, morality, skill-based education, discipline, integrity, and patriotism

A Thriving Learning Environment

Our state-of-the-art facilities and conducive learning spaces provide an atmosphere where creativity and curiosity thrive. We aim to create an environment that stimulates the joy of learning and inspires students to explore new ideas fearlessly.

Parent-Teacher Partnerships

We understand the importance of strong parent-teacher partnerships in a child's education. We actively involve parents in their child's learning journey through regular communication, parent-teacher conferences, and workshops, fostering a supportive and collaborative learning environment.
